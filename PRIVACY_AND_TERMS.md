## 1. Privacy Policy (GDPR Compliance)

At Recode Studio, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data we collect during the licensing process.

### 1.1 Data Collection
To activate and validate the **Pro Edition** of Modbus Manager, we collect and store the following technical information:
*   **Machine ID**: A unique hardware fingerprint generated from your system components (e.g., MAC address, Motherboard ID).
*   **Computer Name**: The hostname of the device.
*   **IP Address**: The IP address from which the activation request originates.
*   **License Key**: The unique activation code assigned to you.

### 1.2 Purpose of Processing
This data is processed **exclusively** for the following purposes:
*   Generating a unique, hardware-locked license key.
*   Verifying the validity of the license during software startup (Anti-Piracy).
*    Preventing unauthorized simultaneous use of the same license on multiple machines.

### 1.3 Data Storage & Sharing
*   **Storage**: Data is stored securely on our private servers located in Europe.
*   **No Third-Party Sharing**: We **DO NOT** sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties. Your information is used strictly for internal license management.

### 1.4 Your Rights
Under GDPR, you have the right to request access to the data we hold about your license or request its deletion (which will result in the deactivation of your license). For such requests, please contact: `info@recodestudio.it`.
